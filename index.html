About Maitree

Maitree is a community dedicated to fostering open-source research and development in the field of Artificial Intelligence (AI) for the Bengali language. We aim to empower researchers, developers, and enthusiasts by providing resources, tools, and collaboration opportunities to advance the state of Bengali NLP and AI.

Our Activities

  • Developing open-source large language models (LLMs) and other AI tools for Bengali
  • Conducting research in various areas of Bengali NLP and AI
  • Organizing workshops, seminars, and hackathons to promote knowledge sharing and collaboration
  • Providing resources and support to the Bengali AI community

Get Involved

We welcome anyone interested in contributing to the advancement of Bengali AI. Here are some ways you can get involved:

  • Join our online community forum
  • Contribute code to our open-source projects
  • Participate in our workshops and events
  • Spread the word about Maitree
